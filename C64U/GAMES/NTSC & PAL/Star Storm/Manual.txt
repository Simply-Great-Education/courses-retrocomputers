Star Storm

Originally developed in 1987, this classic space shooter was lovingly reconstructed from surviving assets when the original was lost. Built in full assembly/machine code, this faithful recreation captures the nostalgic essence of 80s arcade action!



Controls:

Joystick Port 2

Keyboard:

Z - Left
X - Right
Right Shift - Fire


PAL. 8580 (new SID).

Distributed with the Commodore 64 Ultimate with permission from Jim "Viper" Williams.
