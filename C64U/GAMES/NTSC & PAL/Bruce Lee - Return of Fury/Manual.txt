THE STORY SO FAR

35 years later - the Evil Emperor has again risen to power. Only one man can save the day, and we all know who. Bruce's long-time foes, the green Yamo and the black Ninja has been recruited once more, and they are as always hell-bent on foiling any progress Bruce might make in his attempt to overthrow the Evil Emperor.


CONTENTS

- RETURN OF FURY + - a bug-fixed and enhanced version of Return of Fury.
- A version with alternative sprites by TIX.
- No intros.
- Selectable character colours.
- Easy / Hard modes.
- Competition mode.
- Trainers - weak, not like water.
- 3 player action using a 4 player adapter.
- The amazing loader picture by Rune Spaans set to a haunting, previously unused tune by Anders Rodahl (aka. Rage) from 1993.


Note: In-game: RUN/STOP to pause. RESTORE to go back to title.
