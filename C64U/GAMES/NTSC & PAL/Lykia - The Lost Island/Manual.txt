Lykia - The Lost Island

An adventure game by Puls4r in 2022


Do you want to experience something magical in your life? Perhaps a quest that will open your eyes to a world of wonderment, mystery and the values of friendship? If you do, then you have something in common with Nora. But be warned, it won't be easy?




How to play

Use a joystick in port 1 or 2 or the keys E,D,O,P to move Nora. 
Use the joystick button or press comma to use the currently selected item.

Some actions have a long press functionality! 

Press the space key to open the inventory.
To select and activate an inventory item, press comma or the joystick button.
Long press on an inventory item to get a description of the selected item.

To progress through the story you need to use inventory items on people/places.

Press Run/Stop to pause the game, load a save game or quit.

Press button or comma to talk to people, inspect things or to use the currently activated inventory item.

Press F1 to get a hint.

Gomez follows you through the game to save your progress after you complete the very first quest.
