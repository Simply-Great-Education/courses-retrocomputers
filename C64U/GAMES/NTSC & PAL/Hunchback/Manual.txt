The object of the game is to rescue Esmerelda from her castle stronghold, avoiding all the obstacles in Quasimodo's path. The program runs on the Commodore 64 machine.


PLAYING
-------
QUASIMODO must rescue the imprisoned Esmerelda from the castle stronghold. Jump along the Ramparts, swing over the Fiery pit, dodge the Arrows and Fireballs and remember to keep ahead of the chasing Knight. 15 Screens of fun and excitement and each one more difficult than the last.


STATUS AND SCORING 
------------------
On screen scoring shows number of lives, current score and bonus score; 
points are awarded for each completed screen and the bonus builds up if no lives are lost. 
Hall of Fame feature for best score. 


CONTROLS 
--------
Game is controlled by Joystick or Keyboard.
