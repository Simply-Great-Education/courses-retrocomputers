Tiny Harvest - Welcome to the farm!
(c) Artex Software - www.artexsoft.com

In this cozy farming game, you have to help your cousin to restore an old farm. When you arrive, there's a dog, a lonely cow and some barren fields. How will it look after you got to work?

Plant crops, harvest wheat, milk the cows, buy some chickens and collect some eggs. Befriend the dog, bake a cake, and try to get awarded the Best Farm prize! How exactly you do all that is entirely up to you.

The game should explain itself, bit by bit, if you're willing to experiment a little. You can only carry one item at a time, and you only need one button (or alternatively the space bar) for your actions. An interactive hint shows you the options depending on your position.

Some controls & tips

* Use the sign post on the farm (bottom left) to save or load a game from one of 3 slots.
* Use the + / - keys to increase or decrease contrast.
* Use the W A S D keys or the arrow keys to move around. Or use a joystick in port #2.
* Cows and chickens each consume 1 wheat per day. If there's none left, e.g. because you planted all of it, the cows won't give milk and the chickens won't lay any eggs.
* After some time, crops might start to rot and disappear.
* You can never fully mess things up or run out of options. Wait for cherries to replenish your cash.
