Flash XI was released on 11 October 1985.


Flash Cracking Group was founded in April 1985.

Check out more of their releases at https://csdb.dk/group/?id=2163


Note: Flash XI released in 11 October 1985 was the first C64 production to overcome the "upper/lower borders".
It was first presented on the Commodore Fair in Frankfurt, Germany in 1985.

Distributed with the Commodore 64 Ultimate with permission from Duke.
