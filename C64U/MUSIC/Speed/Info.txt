SPEED

by Onslaught, SHAPE, Creators

A collection of multispeed tunes that push the SID chip to its limits.


Setup Requirements: PAL only, JiffyDOS disabled, Drive B disabled. 

Important: Insert Side 2 when prompted after program loading - the program continues running and expects this disk swap.


Distributed with the Commodore 64 Ultimate with permission from Jazzcat/Onslaught.
