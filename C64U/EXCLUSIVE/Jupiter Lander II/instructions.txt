-= JUPITER LANDER 2: JUPITER
FRACT/URE =- 

The official sequel to the first ever
Commodore 64 game! 



-= WELCOME, COMMANDER =- 

On 29th April 1994, something strange
shook the skies. 

*** A bLiNdInG FlAsH! *** 

Then Jupiter was gone. 

Splintered. 

Shattered. 

Ripped from reality itself. 

What remains is a maze of floating
islands - Titanic chunks of the
once-mighty gas and ice planet
drifting through a storm of vapor and
lightning. The world calls the event
"The Blip". Science offers no
answers. But humanity rushed in,
building ships to explore the broken
planet. 

You are the last surviving pilot. The
answers lie with you. Navigate the
chaos. Dock on fragments. Survive the
storms. Collect as much data as you
can to unlock the mystery. The legacy
is yours to fly. 

Welcome to Jupiter Lander 2: Jupiter
Fract/ure. 




-= SETTINGS =- 

- PAL only (for now) 
- 8580 SID preferred 
- Set the addresses for your SID
chips or UltiSIDs as follows: 
SID1 at $d400 
SID2 at $df00 

If using the "RunMeFirst.cfg" file
(recommended!), the SID settings will
be set automatically. 

Note: this config will use UltiSid
and disable real SID. If you have one
or two real SIDs installed, manually
configure as per above settings and
do not use the config file. 



-= CONTROLS =- 

Joystick Port 2 

Keyboard 
W - Thrust 
A - Left 
D - Right 
Return - Thrust 
Space - Pause 

Splash screen: 
Fire/Return/Space to exit 

Main menu: 
Fire/Return to start 
F1 for help
